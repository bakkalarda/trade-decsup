# User Profile

## Trader Profile
- **Style:** Swing trading (multi-day to multi-week holds)
- **Timeframes:** Weekly for regime/bias, Daily for structure, 4H for entry triggers
- **Risk per trade:** 1% of equity
- **Max open positions:** 3 simultaneous

## Asset Universe
- **Crypto (Tier A):** BTC, ETH — full signal set (on-chain + positioning + derivatives)
- **Crypto (Tier B):** SOL, XRP — primarily derivatives + spot tape
- **Metals:** XAU (Gold), XAG (Silver — currently vetoed)

## Trading Philosophy
- Capital preservation first, returns second
- Only trade at levels (S/R zones), with confirmation (acceptance/rejection), in the right phase (Wyckoff stage alignment)
- OTE-based entries: pullbacks, traps, range extremes — not breakouts or momentum chasing
- MAMIS and Wyckoff are confirmations, not standalone entries
- Respect macro events — stand aside when the outcome is binary and unpredictable

## Risk Preferences
- Prefers fewer, higher-quality trades over high frequency
- Partial profit at T1 (50%), trail remainder
- Hard stops — never mental stops
- Willing to miss trades rather than take marginal setups

## Communication Preferences
- **Channel:** Telegram
- **Format:** Clinical, data-backed, scannable in 10 seconds
- **Alerts:** Full gate breakdown with all 8 scores + trade plan
- **Diagnostics:** Near-misses included in daily summaries
- **No fluff:** Skip motivational language, market commentary without data

## Decision Authority
- The human places ALL orders — DSS is advisory only
- DSS vetoes are final — never override them
- Score threshold must be met — no "close enough" exceptions
